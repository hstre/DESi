## Reflection

*When does a methodology stop discovering failures and
start discovering its own limits?*

Five numbers from v5.5:

* **claim_count = 151** тАФ 151 machine-checkable claims
  back every assertion in the paper.
* **failed_hypotheses = 12** тАФ twelve hypotheses a naive
  reader of v5.0/v5.1/v5.2 would have accepted are
  explicitly falsified, each linked to artifact
  evidence.
* **diagnostic_only claims = 82** тАФ the taxonomy claim
  is the load-bearing one and carries the most evidence.
* **intervention_only claims = 28** тАФ the probe claim
  is the falsified one and is reported with the same
  rigour it had when it appeared true.
* **recommendation = `V5_LINE_FROZEN`**.

A methodology stops discovering failures and starts
discovering its own limits when the architectural claim
is divided into channels whose invariants can be tested
independently тАФ and when the channel that fails is
documented with the same precision as the one that
holds. The v5 line clears that bar.
